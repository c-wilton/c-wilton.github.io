My music history

Main musical activity is Batala, more of this can be read below. Musical activities started with guitar, keyboard, clarinet, drum kit, saxophone. In 2019 and after 15 years of being in Batala, I was finally able to go to Brazil, and visit Salvador Da Bahia where our music originates from. Joining Batala has been a huge influence on my life.
