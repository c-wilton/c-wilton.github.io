My music history

I have been musical my life as I come from a musical family. My musical activities started with playing the guitar, keyboard, and clarinet, then a bit later in life, the drum kit and saxophone.

BATALA

In 2006 I joined Batala, a worldwide Samba Reggae drumming group. With Batala I have done many gigs and events both locally and worldwide. In 2019 and after 15 years of being in Batala, I was finally able to go to Brazil, and visit Salvador Da Bahia where our music originates from. Joining Batala has been a huge influence on my life.
